Pentagon Tiler

1. Select a shape type below.
2. Click canvas to spawn a shape.
3. Drag to move shapes.
4. Rotate: Hold 'Q' or 'E', or use the Mouse Wheel to rotate a shape.
5. Press 'F' to Flip (Reflect).
6. Press 'C' to Cycle Colors.
7. Press 'Delete' to remove selected.
*Try to cover the screen without gaps!
